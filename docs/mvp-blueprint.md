# PastorCRM MVP Blueprint

## Product Summary

PastorCRM is a church relationship platform focused on pastoral care, member engagement, attendance tracking, communication, and internal church operations. The MVP supports administrative workflows, community-building features inside church groups, AI office assistance, sermon distribution, and multi-channel communication automation.

## Core MVP Modules

### 1. Member Directory

- Member profiles with names, phone numbers, birthdays, address, email, and family/household links
- Family relationships such as spouse, parent, child, guardian, and sibling
- Notes with visibility controls for staff-only and leader-visible content
- Tags and audience filters for ministry, age group, team, or communication lists

### 2. Attendance

- Attendance tracking for worship services, Sunday school classes, small groups, and ministry events
- Attendance trends on member profiles
- Missed-attendance signals for follow-up

### 3. Pastoral Care

- Prayer requests with status and assignee
- Follow-up reminders for pastors and staff
- Visit tracking for home, hospital, counseling, outreach, and phone visits
- Task dashboard for overdue and upcoming care items
- Weekly or biweekly prayer-request text digests from approved lists

### 4. Messaging

- Bulk SMS for announcements
- Bulk SMS for prayer-request updates
- Automated birthday SMS
- Calendar-event reminder texts
- Email newsletters and church announcements through Mailchimp integration
- Message templates and delivery tracking
- Opt-in / opt-out support and communication history

### 5. Dashboards

- Birthdays today
- Open follow-ups
- Recent attendance patterns
- Open prayer requests
- Recent visits
- Recent message campaign performance

### 6. Calendars

- Internal staff calendars for pastors, ministry leaders, secretaries, and employees
- Public church calendars for official events and ministry schedules
- Unlimited named calendars per church
- Personal absence and time-off tracking
- Shared ministry coordination across departments
- Calendar ownership and visibility rules
- Automatic event reminder texts one day before and two hours before

### 7. AI Office Assistant

- Built-in church secretary assistant for documents and repetitive office tasks
- Meeting notes generated from uploaded one- to two-hour meeting audio
- Sermon notes extracted from uploaded PowerPoint presentations
- Saved templates for letters, certificates, bulletins, and ministry documents

### 8. Sermon Distribution

- Store sermon series, sermon notes, slide files, and attachments
- Target sermon content to different audiences such as church-wide members, youth, kids ministry, worship team, staff, or selected user roles
- Send sermon follow-up by text or email to the people who need to receive it
- Support internal-only sermon resources for pastors, worship leaders, or staff teams

## Purchased Daily Media

The church can use commercially licensed daily image bundles and reel bundles from an outside supplier instead of generating them with AI.

- import 365 images as numbered daily assets
- import 365 reels as numbered daily assets
- use the image of the day for social feed posts
- use the same image of the day for stories when supported
- use the reel of the day for daily short-form video posting
